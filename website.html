Our Values

Innovation

Recogni’s innovative approach in high resolution imaging and perception processing with low power consumption footprint democratizes driving autonomy and its widespread adoption.

Safety

Recogni’s zero lag, high performing perception processing is a core attribute to “better-than-human” autonomous vehicle safety

Efficiency

Recogni’s novel combined depth sensing and perception processing with low system overhead obviates existing complex GPU/CPU/DSP based systems.
